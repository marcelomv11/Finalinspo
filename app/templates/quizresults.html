Quality of life is an overarching term for the quality of the various domains in life. It is a standard level that consists of the expectations of an individual or society for a good life. The Human Development Index is the most commonly used measure of development. In more simple terms, quality of life is the degree to which an individual is healthy, comfortable, and able to participate in or enjoy life events.
